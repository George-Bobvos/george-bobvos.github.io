02 About

A practice, not a performance

Yoga, without pressure.

I am Filipa, Pipa to friends. Yoga has shaped my life for the past five years; it is no longer a choice, it is a commitment. I am here to grow a small community of people who want to move better, breathe deeper, and feel more at home in themselves. Just as I do.

My classes centre on Vinyasa, where each movement is guided by the breath. We move slowly, breathe deeply, and build strength. No perfect shapes. No rushing. Just space to listen, to reset, and to build a practice that feels like your own.

04 Training

Trained with care

Rooted in ancient yoga.

I completed my 200-hour Yoga Teacher Training in India, through a Yoga Alliance approved program. My teachers came from a small Himalayan village called Tungnath, home to the world's highest Shiva temple. That month shaped how I teach: more focused on guiding, adjusting, and helping you go deeper with the breath. The work draws on Hatha, Ashtanga Vinyasa, Yin, Pranayama, meditation, anatomy, philosophy, and Ayurveda.

06 Your teacher

Hi, I am Filipa

A little more about myself.

I am 31, born in Lisbon, Portugal. I have lived in Croatia, Belgium, and the Netherlands, and each place shaped me in its own way. Now happily in Barcelona, I have fallen in love with the small neighbourhood of Poblenou: its rhythm, its beach vibe, and the way people here care about how they live.

My day-to-day life is corporate, and outside of work I am a yogi. Yoga is how I take care of myself, and one of the ways I most love connecting with others.
