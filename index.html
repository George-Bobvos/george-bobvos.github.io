Pipa Yoga

A soft place to land.

Pipa Yoga is not about perfect shapes or pushing harder. It is about making space: in the body, in the breath, and in the day.

Sessions are calm, personal, and easy to enter. You move, breathe, pause, reset, and slowly build a practice that feels like yours.

Training foundation

Traditional roots, modern life.

Filipa completed a 200-hour Yoga Teacher Training with Yogagau, a Yoga Alliance approved school. Her training included Hatha, Ashtanga Vinyasa, Yin Yoga, pranayama, meditation, anatomy, alignment, yoga philosophy, Ayurveda, and teaching methodology.

The heart of her teaching is simple: yoga should help you live better, not perform better.

Meet your teacher

Filipa Mourão Martinho

I am Filipa, Portuguese, and I have lived in Poblenou for two years. My day-to-day life is corporate, and outside work I am a yogi, certified through a 200-hour Yoga Teacher Training recognised by Yoga Alliance.

Yoga is not only something I teach. It is how I take care of my own life, and one of the ways I most enjoy connecting with people.

Poblenou has slowly become home: its rhythm, its mix, and the care many people here put into how they live. What excites me now is growing a small, real community in the neighbourhood: neighbours, friends, and people who share a more conscious way of living.
