Meet your teacher

Hi, I’m Filipa.

I’m Portuguese and I’ve lived in Poblenou for two years. My day-to-day life is corporate, and outside work I’m a yogi.

Yoga is how I take care of myself. It is also one of the ways I most enjoy connecting with people.

I’ve fallen in love with Poblenou: its rhythm, its mix, and the care people here put into how they live. I’d love to grow a small community around that same feeling.
