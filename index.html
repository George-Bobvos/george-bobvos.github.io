04 Training

Trained with care

A foundation in many traditions.

Completed my 200-hour Yoga Teacher Training in India in March 2026 — a Yoga Alliance approved program. The month there shaped how I teach: slower, more curious, more in love with the breath. The work draws on Hatha, Ashtanga Vinyasa, Yin, Pranayama, meditation, anatomy and philosophy.
