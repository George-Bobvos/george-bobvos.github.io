Meet your teacher

Hi, I’m Filipa.

I’m Portuguese and I’ve lived in Poblenou for two years. My day-to-day life is corporate, and in my free time I’m a yogi.

Yoga is not just something I teach. It is how I take care of my own life, and one of the ways I most enjoy connecting with people.

I’ve fallen in love with this neighbourhood: its rhythm, its mix, and the way people here care about how they live. I’d love to grow a small community around that same feeling.
