Your teacher

Filipa Mourão Martinho

Portuguese. Poblenou based. 200h YTT certified.

Yoga is how I care for my own life, and one of the ways I most enjoy connecting with people.

I am Filipa, Portuguese, and I have lived in Poblenou for two years. My day-to-day life is corporate, and outside work I am a yogi, certified through a 200-hour Yoga Teacher Training recognised by Yoga Alliance.

For me, yoga is not only something I teach. It is how I return to myself, how I take care of my body and mind, and how I build meaningful connection with others.

Since moving to Poblenou, I have fallen in love with the neighbourhood: its rhythm, its mix of people, and the care many locals put into how they live. What excites me now is growing a small, genuine community here, meeting neighbours and friends who share a more conscious way of living.
